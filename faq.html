Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Resonix

To become a member of Resonix Society, you need to:

  • Be enrolled in any domain of btech
  • Fill out the membership application form on our contact page
  • Attend an orientation session

We welcome students from all academic years and encourage active participation in our activities.

Currently, basic membership is free for all students. However, there may be nominal fees for:

  • Workshop materials and equipment usage
  • Field trips and industry visits

Resonix Society members enjoy numerous benefits:

  • Access to exclusive workshops and technical sessions
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Priority registration for events and competitions
  • Access to project development resources
  • Mentorship from senior members and faculty
  • Certificate of participation and achievements
  • Career guidance and placement support
  • Opportunity to lead and organize events

Registration for our events is simple:

  1. Visit the Events page on our website
  2. Browse upcoming events and workshops
  3. Click on the event you're interested in
  4. Fill out the registration form
  5. You'll receive a confirmation email with event details

Early registration is recommended as seats are limited for most workshops.

Most of our events are open to all students, whether they are members or not. However:

  • Members get priority registration
  • Some exclusive workshops are member-only
  • Certification is available only for students

We organize a diverse range of events throughout the year:

  • Technical Workshops: Hands-on sessions on latest technologies
  • Seminars: Expert talks on industry trends and innovations
  • Webinars: Online sessions with industry professionals
  • Competitions: Technical contests and hackathons
  • Project Showcases: Platform to present student projects
  • Industry Visits: Tours to tech companies and research labs
  • Career Fairs: Networking events with potential employers

We love showcasing innovative student projects! To submit your project:

  1. Go to our Contact page and select "Submit Project"
  2. Fill out the project submission form with details
  3. Include project description, technologies used, and team members
  4. Provide links to documentation, code repository, or demo videos
  5. Our review team will evaluate your submission
  6. Selected projects will be featured on our website and social media

We do not provide material resources; however, we extend comprehensive support and guidance to students in the development of their projects

Our society operates with the following schedule:

  • Office Hours: Monday-Friday, 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Laboratory Access: Monday-Firday, 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM (for members)
  • Events: Flexible timing based on event schedule
  • Emergency Contact: Available via email 24/7

Stay connected with Resonix Society through multiple channels:

  • Website: Check our news and events pages regularly
  • Newsletter: Subscribe to our monthly newsletter
  • Social Media: Follow us on Instagram, and LinkedIn
  • Email Notifications: Automatic updates for registered members
  • WhatsApp Groups: Instant updates and discussions
  • Notice Boards: Physical announcements in notice and department
